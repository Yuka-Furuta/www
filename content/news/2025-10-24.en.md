Space Cubics will exhibit its products at [NIHONBASHI SPACE WEEK 2025
EXHIBITION][1], held at Nihonbashi Mitsui Hall October 28–30, 2025
(Tue–Thu).

At the Space Cubics section of the STARTUP HOKKAIDO (Hokkaido /
Hokkaido Bureau of Economy, Trade and Industry) booth, we will
showcase [SC-OBC Module A1][2], a computer module for nano- and
microsatellites, along with a new high-end on-board computer (OBC) for
the New Space sector, scheduled for announcement in November 2025.

We look forward to seeing you at the event.
