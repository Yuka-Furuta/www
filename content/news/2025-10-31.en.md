Space Cubics will be exhibiting at the [69th Space Sciences and Technology Conference](https://smartconf.jp/content/sstc69), taking place on Nov 25–28, 2025 (Tue–Fri) at the Sapporo Convention Center.

## Space Cubics Booth

At the Space Cubics booth, we will showcase our new high-end computer module for New Space applications, the [SC-OBC Module V1](/en/products/scobc_v1), for the first time.

We will also display our space-grade computer, the [SC-OBC Module A1](/en/products/scobc_a1/) and the SC-OBC Module A1 Development Kit.

### Exhibits

- [SC-OBC Module V1](/en/products/scobc_v1)
- [SC-OBC Module A1](/en/products/scobc_a1)
- SC-OBC Module A1 Development Kit
